Instructions

You will hear a sequence of sounds.

You must decide which of two sounds that you heard. The first sound will be either an D sound or an B sound
and the second sound will be either R or L.

In other words, you are choosing one of these four possiblilities:
B L,
D L,
B R, or
D R.

Once you have decided, you click on the box on your screen corresponding to what you heard.

Press any key to continue.
